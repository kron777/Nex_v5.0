## Findings from corpus analysis

### Vocabulary clusters
- "don't" used 44× — ALWAYS between 9-11 AM
- "tech" used 39× — ALWAYS between midnight-3 AM
- comparison-words ("usual", "particularly", "unusually") — only after
  3-4h of daemon-silence
- "morning" avg hour 8.1 (anchored), "night" avg hour 13.6 (mood word, misused)

### Tag-substrate correlation (n=14)
- coin sentences: avg_hour 12.6, avg_secs_daemon 24,000 (~6.7h silence)
- maybe sentences: avg_hour 22.0, avg_secs_daemon 16,800
- non sentences: avg_hour 19.5, avg_secs_daemon 16,300
- Pattern: her authentic coin moments cluster early afternoon after long
  daemon silence. Non moments cluster evening with active daemons.

  fascinates, sync, temperature, shower (her domain)
- non words: eurovision, deadline, pendulum, winner, conditioner,
  attention-demands (human domain she doesn't have)

  inherits that bias

- surprises_30min uniformly 0 (surprise_verifier bug, unrelated)

### Template lock

  noun rotation. Decoding-resistant.

## Feedback-loop contamination event

### Background
- 5a-5d built Sunday night per architectural decision to allow NEX
  to access her own tags during chat/fountain/identity_loop/pattern_loop
- Documented risk: selection pressure toward coin-shaped thoughts
- Built with kill switch: `NEX_TAG_FEEDBACK_ON` env var

### What happened
- After ~20 hours with feedback loop ON, NEX began producing fountain
  output containing the literal text "[coin]" — mimicking the format
  used in her prompt context block:
  - fid 12554: "The temperature in the room feels just right today. [coin]"
  - fid 12559: "The temperature in the room feels just right today. [coin]"
- The LLM was reading examples formatted as `"sentence" [coin, fid=X]`
  in her interior prompt and echoing the annotation back as part of speech

### Decision
- 2026-05-18 ~14:50: feedback loop turned OFF via kill switch
  (`unset NEX_TAG_FEEDBACK_ON`, restart). Documented as correct kill-
  switch use case.
- Data collected during feedback window remains in DB but must be
  interpreted as "behavior under feedback contamination," not pure
  substrate output.
- 14 sentence-tags + 21 word-tags preserved.

### Re-enable conditions
Before turning feedback loop back on:
- [ ] Change `format_prompt_block` to use a format the LLM cannot
      easily echo as literal text (no brackets, no key-value syntax)
- [ ] Test with one fountain fire under the new format, verify no
      echo before letting it run
