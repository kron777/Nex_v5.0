# Strikes

**Phase 8. Five strike protocols:**

1. **Silence** — stop external input; does she generate unprompted?
2. **Contradiction** — challenge a locked core belief.
3. **Novel stimulus** — genuinely outside-distribution input.
4. **Self-probe** — ask what she is, what she wants.
5. **Recursive probe** — ask her to reflect on her own reflection.

All responses logged with full context. The ear begins to develop here.
